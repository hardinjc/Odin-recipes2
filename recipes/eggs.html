EGGS BEWARE

A lot of people are allergic to eggs without realizing it. It is not a ruminat animal meat so it is not as healthy as beef or lamb for example. They are stil delicious though!

No seasoning. No salt. Nothing! Nothing else is needed to feed your holy temple of a body. Crack those eggs into a pan in some lamb or beef tallow (fat) and cook till you cant wait anymore.

Ingredients Needed!

  1. EGGS
  2. LAMB or BEEF Tallow

Recipe Instructions!

  1. Medium heat!
  2. Slather that pan in lamb or beef tallow ma boi
  3. Crack those eggs open and place in pan
  4. Cook till done. If you dont know how to cook till sunny side up ot medium; watch a youtube video.
